Practical Hypermedia APIs

with Matthew Reinbold

Hypermedia APIs, or APIs powered by links in the same way the web is, are a source of developer contention. While they promise to minimize the coupling between an API and app the theory doesn't quite match the reality.

Matthew will go over how we got here, what hypermedia proposes to do, why that's important, and best ideas going forward.
